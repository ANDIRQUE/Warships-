When it comes to technology news, you could say SlashGear has always been ahead of the curve.

Originally launched by Ewdison Then and Vincent Nguyen in 2005 (two years before anyone had heard of an iPhone) as a blog covering the mobile tech industry, the brand quickly made a name for itself with its timely coverage of smartphone news throughout the mid aughts. We believe our longevity is a testament to our continued goal of serving as the ultimate resource for those who want to stay up to date on the latest and greatest tech on the market. And while we do write about the business end of things, we distinguish ourselves by focusing our coverage on the consumer, keeping practicality in mind as well as the luxury and wow factor that makes each product compelling for users
